News and Futures

Chapter 1, Section 2

This section will include information regarding news and futures. These topics are vital to the success of technical traders. Understanding news and how it affects a stock's movement is crucial to comprehend random fluctuations of a stocks price. Additionally, futures are another part of the puzzle to determine where a stock might move. These come as almost replicas of certain ETFs to help predict the next move.

The following topics will be covered in this section:

  • news;
  • futures;
  • short selling;
  • dip buying;
  • inverse effect;
  • correlated ETFs.
