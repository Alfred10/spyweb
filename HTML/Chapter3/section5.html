Conclusion

This chapter is an important chapter to understand. The first two chapters covers the basic technicals you need to understand to begin to trade. Chapter 3 discusses some technicals to add on to but ultimately brings concepts to mind with your trading. This isn't discussed a lot in the trading industry so it's very important to fully understand what was said here. A lot of trading success doesn't go into the executions themselves. It goes into the transparency and self awareness with developing your style of trading.

What I've found is you learn through building up your process. The constant reviews, adjustments and rules will only advance your trading. Also, trading is recognized as a mirror of your daily life. If you can't follow a daily plan to wake up, go to the gym, exercise, meditate, etc. on a specified regimen, what makes you think you can follow a trading plan. As time goes on, and your experience with trading develops, this will begin to show more truth. I hope everyone reading this realizes the reality of trading through this education. Read carefully, print out the PDF and have fun with it!

Key takeaways:

  • Don't sell unless there is a reason to sell;
  • Have a plan with every trade;
  • Focus on one quality trade and repeat;
  • More patience, more reward;
  • Trade the chart, not your emotion.
