SPYweb v:2.0.0

This is the second version of SPYweb, which was released on 02/17/2019. The update contains various additions and changes to the content and styling of the website. The most significant change in this update is the addition of Chapter 2.

Additions:

  • New chapter (Chapter 2).
  • About Us page.
  • Contact Us page.
  • What's New page.
  • Footer at the bottom of each page.
  • Quiz at the end of Chapter 2.
  • More JavaScript.

Changes:

  • Minor corrections to Chapter 1.
  • Small adjustments to the formatting and styling of each section.
  • New design for the Home page.
  • Website name in the navigation bar.
  • Different color scheme for navigation bar.
